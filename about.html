About

Small team, serious about the messy problems.

Haphazard Solutions is a boutique consultancy built on a simple idea: the tangled, "where do we even start" problems are the ones most worth solving.

Our story

We started Haphazard Solutions after one too many projects that were stuck not for lack of talent, but for lack of clarity. The work was scattered, the priorities kept shifting, and nobody had the time to step back and make sense of it all. That's the gap we fill.

How we work

  • Clarity first. We start by making the problem legible before proposing solutions.
  • Hands-on. We work in the details with your team, not from a distance.
  • Leave it better. Success means you don't need us anymore.
  • Straight talk. Honest assessments, even when they're inconvenient.
