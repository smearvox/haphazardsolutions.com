◈ OPERATIONAL INTELLIGENCE CONSULTING

What your organization knows — and how well it knows it.

Your data, your institutional knowledge, and the AI you point at both. We treat a valid inference as seriously as a working pipeline — a confident answer built on a broken one is worse than no answer at all.

Data risk profile & remediation — surface your data-related exposures and a prioritized plan to close them.
