Services

Two practices, one standard of rigor.

Haphazard Solutions works across two consulting practices — operational intelligence and operational effectiveness. Engagements are scoped to your problem, not a fixed package.

Operational Intelligence Consulting

What your organization knows — and how well it knows it.

Your data, your institutional knowledge, and the AI you apply to both. We care as much about whether your inferences are valid as whether your pipelines run.

Data

Strategy & risk

  • Data strategy review — a clear-eyed read on how your data is (and isn't) serving the business, and where to invest next.
  • AI strategy & risk review — where AI genuinely fits your operations, and an honest accounting of the risks that come with it.
  • Data risk profile & remediation — surface your data-related exposures and a prioritized plan to close them.

Governance

  • Governance, compliance & security — policies and controls that keep data trustworthy, lawful, and protected.

Architecture & engineering

  • Data architecture & database design — data models and systems that fit how your data actually flows.
  • Data engineering — pipelines and ETL/ELT that move data reliably from source to use.

Analysis & science

  • Data science & analytics — turning data into answers, from exploratory analysis to production models.

Research rigor

  • Research design (quantitative) — studies built to answer the question you're actually asking.
  • Experimental design — properly controlled experiments; the real thing, not just A/B splits.
  • Inferential validity — making sure the conclusions your analysis supports are the ones you're actually drawing.
  • Quality reviews & adversarial cross-validation — independent, deliberately skeptical scrutiny of findings before you bet on them.

Culture

  • Data literacy & democratization — strategy to make sound data practice something your whole organization can do, not just the specialists.

Operational Effectiveness Consulting

Making the organization itself work better.

Its strategy, its methods, and its resilience when things go wrong.

  • Organizational strategy review — an outside read on where you're headed and whether your structure supports it.
  • Methodological fit reviews — which methodology actually suits your firm — Lean, Six Sigma, Skunk Works, a given project-management style — both as you are now and as you aspire to be.
  • Process modeling & auditing — map how work really happens, then find where it breaks or drags.
  • Risk analysis — identify what could go wrong and what it would cost you.
  • FMEA — structured failure mode & effects analysis: how processes fail, and what each failure would do.
  • Contingency planning & business impact analysis — know what you'd do when disruption hits, and what's most critical to protect.
  • Lean vs. Robust auditing & recalibration — find where you've traded resilience for efficiency (or vice versa), and rebalance deliberately.
